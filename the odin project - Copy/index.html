Ethiopia: History and Nature

Ethiopia is a unique East African country known for its ancient history, diverse cultures, and stunning landscapes. Never colonized, it has preserved deep traditions. From historic churches to mountain peaks, and as the birthplace of coffee, Ethiopia offers a rich and authentic experience.

Some random information

A dramatic mountain range known for hiking, wildlife, and stunning views.

Famous for its 12th-century rock-hewn churches, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

One of the hottest and most otherworldly places on Earth, with colorful volcanic landscapes.

An ancient city known for obelisks, ruins, and its link to the Queen of Sheba.
